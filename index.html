Teams

  • Mail Routing

    Spam is only one of the challenges when working with millions of emails a day. Using a lot of Linux, open source technologies, and a variety of programming languages, we make sure that mail gets delivered on time!

  • Mail Storage

    No one ever, ever, EVER wants to lose an email, so we go crazy about large-scale storage and redundancy. Using Dovecot, Python, C, and high-density servers you'll have that mail when you need it!

  • Anti-Abuse

    We are a back-end team writing software to protect 4 million email mailboxes from abuse. 100% Linux DevOps, writing everything in Python unless we need to use C. Help us stop the bad guys from destroying a multimillion dollar email platform.

  • Provisioning

    Managing over 350,000 Exchange mailboxes is no small task. We handle 10,000+ updates every day. With C# .Net, MSMQ, PowerShell, and REST APIs we've got the tools to keep it all running smoothly.

  • Webmail

    Businesses need more than email, and that's where Webmail steps up with calendar, contacts, synchronization services, and a sophisticated web interface to rule them all. We keep productivity high with JavaScript, Python, PHP, Redis, and MySQL.

  • Email and Apps Billing & Integration

    Our billing and integration team brings in the money and pulls our products together. We’re laying the foundation for new products and services to easily plug into common functions such as customer, signup, upgrades, billing, pricing, and promotions. We use C#, .NET, VB.NET, IIS, and SQL Server to unite our products and share them with other groups in Rackspace.

  • Email and Apps Control Panel

    Our Control Panel team puts the power at our customers' fingertips with a robust web application and REST API for email management. We practice continuous integration/continuous delivery using tools such as TeamCity and GitHub and use the best technology on the Microsoft stack, including ASP.NET MVC / C#, AngularJS, and SQL Server.

  • Email and Apps Engineering & Infrastructure

    Our systems enable the configuration and billing of all our Cloud Office products. We architect our systems and services to be available 24 X 7. We are a collaborative, DevOps culture that prides itself in refactoring our applications and restructuring our servers, networks and databases to make them always available to our customers. We heavily leverage Windows and C# to make our stuff awesome.

  • Cloud Control Panel

    Fanatical Support starts with a Control Panel that enables our customers to get things done faster and simpler than ever before. You'll work with Javascript, Ruby, and Python to turn our public REST APIs into a responsive and beautiful web interface that leads our customers to rave about their experience with our Cloud products.

  • Directory Synchronization

    Our customers are too busy running their business to manage mailboxes. We've got the tools to keep their email configuration in sync with their active directory. Using C# .Net and REST APIs we make configuring email seamless.

  • Cloud Servers and Images

    We are an Openstack project that provides key services such as registry, discovery, retrieval and management of virtual machine images. We strive to be highly available, extremely fault-tolerant and easily recoverable to be able to cater to the real-time needs of clouds. Combined together, we power the 2nd largest public cloud, Rackspace Cloud, in the industry and see ourself as a key contributor to Openstack. So, this is where all the action is, if 'Python', 'Agile Development', 'Open Source', 'Cloud' and 'Awesome' appeal to you!

About Rackspace

Rackspace® (NYSE: RAX) is the #1 managed cloud company. Its technical expertise and Fanatical Support® allow companies to tap the power of the cloud without the pain of hiring experts in dozens of complex technologies. Rackspace is also the leader in hybrid cloud, giving each customer the best fit for its unique needs—whether on single- or multi-tenant servers, or a combination of those platforms. Rackspace is the founder of OpenStack®, the open-source operating system for the cloud. Headquartered in San Antonio, Rackspace serves more than 300,000 business customers from data centers on four continents.
